Import to CRM

Choose a spreadsheet or phone contacts export. PulseNow will clean, categorize, and place each contact into a CRM area.

Phone contacts need to be exported first Best formats: .vcf, .vCard, or Google CSV

iPhone

  1. Open Contacts and tap Lists.
  2. Touch and hold a list, then tap Export.
  3. Save or send the contact cards, then upload the .vcf file here.

On a computer, iCloud Contacts can also export a vCard.

Google Contacts

  1. Open contacts.google.com and select the contacts.
  2. Use More actions, then Export.
  3. Choose Google CSV or vCard, then upload the file here.

Google CSV keeps spreadsheet columns clear; vCard works well for phone contacts.
